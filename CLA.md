## How to Sign

When outside contributions are open, our CLA bot will comment on Your first pull
request. To sign, post the following as a pull-request comment (exactly):

> I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA

Your GitHub username, the pull-request reference, and a timestamp are then
recorded in the Project's signature ledger. You only sign once; it covers Your
future Contributions. To re-run the check after signing, comment `recheck`.
